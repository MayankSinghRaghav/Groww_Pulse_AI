Action Roadmap
Based on this week's app review data, here are 3 concrete, high-level action ideas for the product team:
1. **Simplify Onboarding Process**: Many users have reported difficulty in navigating the app's initial setup and investment process. To address this, the product team can redesign the onboarding flow to make it more intuitive and user-friendly, reducing the number of steps required to start investing and providing clear, concise guidance throughout the process.
2. **Enhance Portfolio Tracking and Analytics**: Several users have requested more detailed and customizable portfolio tracking features, including real-time updates and more granular analytics. The product team can prioritize the development of advanced portfolio tracking tools, allowing users to better monitor their investments and make more informed decisions.
